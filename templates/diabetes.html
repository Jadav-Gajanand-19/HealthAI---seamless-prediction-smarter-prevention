Diabetes Prediction

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects the way your body processes blood sugar (glucose). When unmanaged, it can cause serious complications like heart disease, kidney damage, and nerve problems.

What Causes Diabetes?

  • Insufficient insulin production or resistance
  • Family history or genetic factors
  • Obesity and lack of physical activity
  • Unhealthy diet and high sugar intake

How Do We Predict It?

Using Machine Learning models trained on medical datasets, we analyze key parameters such as: glucose level, BMI, insulin levels, age, and blood pressure. This helps us predict diabetes risk in a fast and reliable way.

Why Early Detection Matters

Early detection allows individuals to take preventive actions and avoid serious complications. Our system empowers people with personalized prediction and lifestyle advice.

What Inputs Are Collected and Why?

  • Pregnancies: Number of times the patient has been pregnant – used to evaluate gestational diabetes risk.
  • Glucose: Blood sugar level – key indicator of diabetic status.
  • Blood Pressure: High blood pressure can indicate related complications.
  • Skin Thickness: Indicates body fat distribution.
  • Insulin: Insulin levels in the blood – helps assess insulin resistance.
  • BMI: Body Mass Index – obesity is a strong risk factor for diabetes.
  • Diabetes Pedigree Function: A score indicating family history and genetic predisposition.
  • Age: Older age increases risk of developing diabetes.
  • Physical Activity Level: Sedentary lifestyle increases risk.
