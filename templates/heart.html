Heart Disease Risk Prediction

Heart disease is a leading global cause of death. This tool helps assess your risk using machine learning, giving you the chance to take preventive action early.

Why We Ask These Inputs

  • Age: Risk increases as you get older.
  • Sex: Gender can impact risk levels.
  • cp (Chest Pain): Indicates heart stress levels.
  • trestbps: High blood pressure = heart strain.
  • chol: High cholesterol = clogged arteries.
  • fbs: High blood sugar may indicate diabetes.
  • restecg: Detects electrical heart abnormalities.
  • thalach: Max heart rate from activity.
  • exang: Chest pain during exercise.
  • oldpeak: ST depression during stress.
  • slope: Measures stress test results.
  • ca: Number of colored vessels via imaging.
  • thal: Blood disorder affecting oxygen.

How It Works

We use AI models trained on real health data to generate a personalized risk score. It’s fast, secure, and informative — helping you take early action.

Why Early Prediction Matters

Many heart conditions are preventable if detected early. Use this tool to catch early signs and stay ahead with lifestyle changes or medical guidance.
